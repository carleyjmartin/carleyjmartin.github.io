background

Carley's Craft Caf√© makes bespoke and pre-made crochet and amigurumi projects. Carley has many years of experience creating and making crochet patterns. She began to crochet during time spent in Svalbard. Where the long, dark nights meant some free time to explore hobbies and there she taught herself to crochet. She has been hooked (pun intended) ever since.

Amigurumi is the Japanese art of making small stuffed toys. In Japanese, 'ami' means to crochet or knit, and 'nuigurumi' is a stuffed doll. Amigurumi is a modern technique outside of Japan, only really coming into popular culture in the early 2000's.

tools and materials

We strive to use the most environmentally friendly and responsibly sourced wool and synthetics. Each project consultation will go over the pricing structures for different materials and a suitable material will be agreed upon. Please make Carley aware of any allergies or sensitivities during consultations. We are happy to accommodate all and will always be happy for a chance to work with a new material!

If amigurumi is intended for children under 5, be aware that 'show amigurumi' may contain small parts. If the project is expected to be a toy, let Carley know so different parts may be used for safety.
