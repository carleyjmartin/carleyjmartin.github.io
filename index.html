background

Carley's Craft Café makes bespoke and pre-made crochet and amigurumi projects. Carley has many years of experience creating and making crochet patterns. She began to crochet during time spent in Svalbard. Where the long, dark nights meant some free time to explore hobbies and there she taught herself to crochet. She has been hooked (pun intended) ever since.

Amigurumi is the Japanese art of making small stuffed toys. In Japanese, 'ami' means to crochet or knit, and 'nuigurumi' is a stuffed doll. Amigurumi is a modern technique outside of Japan, only really coming into popular culture in the early 2000's.

commissions

Carley takes on commissions on a first come first serve basis as all projects are made by hand and may take several hours of work, if not more. Christmas is a very busy time, so be sure to get your orders in early! Time estimates for projects are given below.

All commissions require 50% deposit on order, with the remaining payment on shipping.

As all projects are bespoke the deposit is non-refundable, however we will keep in touch during production to make sure every detail is to specification. A final look will be available before shipping to make sure the project is perfect for your needs, full payment is required before shipping.

tools and materials

We strive to use the most environmentally friendly and responsibly sourced wool and synthetics. Each project consultation will go over the pricing structures for different materials and a suitable material will be agreed upon. Please make Carley aware of any allergies or sensitivities during consultations. We are happy to accommodate all and will always be happy for a chance to work with a new material!

If amigurumi is intended for children under 5, be aware that 'show amigurumi' may contain small parts. If the project is expected to be a toy, let Carley know so different parts may be used for safety.
