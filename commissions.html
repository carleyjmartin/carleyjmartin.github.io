commissions

Carley takes on commissions on a first come first serve basis as all projects are made by hand and may take several hours of work, if not more. Christmas is a very busy time, so be sure to get your orders in early! Time estimates for projects are given below.

All commissions require 50% deposit on order, with the remaining payment on shipping.

As all projects are bespoke the deposit is non-refundable, however we will keep in touch during production to make sure every detail is to specification. A final look will be available before shipping to make sure the project is perfect for your needs, full payment is required before shipping.
